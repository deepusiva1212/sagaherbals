Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we collect:

  • Name, phone number, and delivery address
  • Email address (optional)
  • Order details and payment method selected
  • Messages sent via WhatsApp for order support

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order updates via WhatsApp
  • Respond to your queries and complaints
  • Improve our products and services

3. Payment Information

We do not store credit/debit card details. All card payments are processed securely by Razorpay. We only record the payment method selected and the order total for our records.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with delivery partners to fulfill your order.

5. Data Storage

Your order data is stored securely in Google Firebase. We retain order information for up to 3 years for business records.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

7. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. Firebase may use minimal cookies for authentication purposes only.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us on WhatsApp or email us at the address listed on our Contact page.
